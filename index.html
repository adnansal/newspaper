If not for that glimpse, Urshela would not have known that a game that was supposed to have begun on Wednesday at 7 p.m. had bled into the wee hours of the next day. The game’s many twists and turns — and there were more to come after Urshela checked the time — had captured his attention and captivated those watching at home, presumably with white knuckles.

In this section we develop two simple but powerful prediction methods: the linear model fit by least squares and the k-nearest-neighbor prediction rule.
